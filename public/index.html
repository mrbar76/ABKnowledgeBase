How AB Brain Works ▶

AB Brain is your personal AI-powered knowledge base and fitness platform. It stores everything — workouts, meals, body metrics, conversations, knowledge, tasks — in a single PostgreSQL database with full-text search.

Architecture: Node.js + Express backend, vanilla JS frontend (PWA), PostgreSQL 16 with 14+ tables, deployed on Railway.

AI Integration: ChatGPT and Claude connect via OpenAPI schemas (Actions/Projects). The coach reads your data, plans workouts, logs meals, and tracks recovery — all through the API.

Data Flow: Bee wearable auto-syncs conversations. Fitbod CSVs import exercises. Coach logs workouts/meals/plans via API. Everything is searchable.

Key Concepts & Terms ▶

Recovery Score — 0-100 composite: sleep (30%), TSB training load (25%), muscle freshness (20%), injuries (10%), nutrition (10%), subjective (5%). Uses EWMA (exponential weighted moving average) like TrainingPeaks.

TSB (Training Stress Balance) — CTL (42-day fitness) minus ATL (7-day fatigue). Load = effort × duration (session-RPE). Labels: fresh (+10 to -10), optimal (-10 to -30), productive (-30 to -60), accumulated fatigue (-60 to -100), overreaching (-100 to -150), danger (<-150). Recovery sessions (walks, yoga at effort ≤4) excluded.

Muscle Freshness — Tracks per-muscle recovery windows. High-effort sessions (7+) need up to 1.5× longer recovery. Uses structured exercises when available, falls back to workout_type.

Daily Plans — Coach creates plans with structured exercises (warmup/main/superset/circuit/finisher). After workout, actual exercises are logged for plan-vs-actual comparison.

Gym Profiles — Equipment sets (Home/Gym/Travel). Coach filters exercises to match your available equipment. Supports 42 equipment categories.

Exercise Catalog — 1069 exercises imported from Fitbod with muscle groups, equipment, and categories. Used for exercise lookup and muscle recovery tracking.

Rings — Train (effort), Fuel (protein + calories + hydration), Recover (sleep + quality). Fill proportionally based on daily targets.

Schema Builder — Generates custom OpenAPI specs. ChatGPT limited to 30 endpoints; Claude unlimited. Presets: Spartan (minimal), Brain (knowledge), All (30), Claude (all).

Smart Intake — GPT-4o-mini auto-classifies raw text into knowledge, tasks, or transcripts.

Architecture Decisions ▶

Vanilla JS (no framework) — Fast, no build step, full control. Single app.js + index.html. PWA-capable with service worker.

PostgreSQL over NoSQL — Relational integrity for linked data (plans→workouts→exercises). Full-text search with tsvector + trigram indexes. JSONB for flexible fields (tags, metadata, planned_exercises).

OpenAPI-first AI integration — ChatGPT/Claude read the same API. Multiple schema variants for different token budgets. Schema builder lets you pick exactly what the AI sees.

Effort × Duration for training load — Session-RPE is more practical than power-meter-based TSS. Works for strength, cardio, and hybrid workouts.

Equipment as JSONB (not TEXT[]) — Gym profile equipment stored as JSON array for flexible querying and coach-friendly formatting.

Daily Plans over Training Plans — Per-day granularity is more flexible than week-long plans. Coach creates/adjusts daily based on recovery, schedule, and goals.

Fitbod exercise names kept exact — "Dumbbell Bench Press" not "DB Bench". Ensures screenshot logs match the catalog. Band resistance labels kept as-is (not converted to lbs).
